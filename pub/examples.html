Write music!

Select on a note on the staff and change the pitch/time value of the notes through the UI. You can also change the time signature of the staff and the notes will be adjusted accordingly. Add and remove as many bars as you need, the staff will add/remove rows for the bars. Try editing the staff below.

Play it!

Now that you've written some music on the staff, click the "Play from Start" button in the UI to play the notes! By default, the speed is 120 BPM for a quarter note, but you can adjust the speed as you need.

Hide the UI

You can also choose to hide the UI if needed! Here is an instance of the library without the UI. You can still select notes to play it and get data of it. Try selecting a note below and press the "Show Note" button to see which note you pressed in the console.

Press the button to view the staff's notes on the console!

Get some data!

You can get the data of the notes on the staff. Press the "VIEW STAFF" button to get all of the notes in the staff as an array
